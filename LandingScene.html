About Us

Established in 1973 by our founder Shri O.P. Vyas, the firm is having a long standing experience and is a multifaceted organization providing a range of professional services in various fields including direct and indirect taxation, auditing, management consultancy, project financing and so on. Currently the firm is having its head office in the city of Mumbai and branch offices in Jaipur. We cater to Indian concerns, MNCs, HNIs and NRIs.
Our firm has a strategic-mix of Chartered Accountants, both experienced and young professionals, dedicated to the delegated task. The composition of team is such that it can handle any size of assignment, at office or at site. The firm always tries to hedge its age by associating young professionals.

What We Do

Statutory Audit

We aim to ensure completeness, accuracy, and fair presentation of information over all audited financial statements.

Internal Audit

We evaluate and improve the effectiveness of risk management, control and corporate governance in an entity.

Dispute Resolution

Our experts are aware of the legal regulations and assist in strategizing tax litigation and explore other dispute resolution avenues.

Direct Taxation

Our domain experts will assist the business in every way to mitigate and ease any taxation challenges backed by thorough analysis and accuracy.

Indirect Taxation

Our indirect team assures the compliances of indirect taxation includes Goods & Service Tax, Service Tax etc.

Finance Advisory

Through precise analysis of the business and its requirements, we provide specialized financial advice that will transform your business.
